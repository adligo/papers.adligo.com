At Adligo, AssociativeArrays are simply [Arrays#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.1.0](Arrays.md) that have values or items that are associated with other [Arrays#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.1.0](Arrays.md) in some fashion.  All 2d,3d and nd [Arrays#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.1.0](Arrays.md) are associated to the other [Arrays#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.1.0](Arrays.md) in their group. 
[Maps#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.3](Maps.md) MAY be implemented as AssociativeArrays, however, [Maps#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.3](Maps.md) are NOT necessarily implemented as AssociativeArrays.

Note:  This definition is significantly different from the following wikipedia article;
[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Associative_array](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Associative_array)

Note:  This definition is NOT synonymous with [Maps#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.3](Maps.md), one notible example of a Map that is NOT implemented using AssociativeArrays is [Java's TreeMap](https://docs.oracle.com/javase/8/docs/api/java/util/TreeMap.html)!
