A collection is simply a group of items which is usually a [List#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.0](../Lists.md) or [Set#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.6](../Sets.md).  However, most data structures are groups of things so it is likely that any data structure in this corpus is a collection.  [Graphs#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.14](Graphs.md) and [Maps#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.3](../Maps.md) are considered collections.
