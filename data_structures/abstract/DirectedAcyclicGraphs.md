In mathematics, particularly graph theory, and computer science, a directed acyclic graph (DAG) is a directed [Graph#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.14](Graphs.md) with no directed [Cycles#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.12.5](NodeNetworks.md#cycles).  DirectedAcyclicGraphs may take the form of a single [NodeNetwork#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.12](NodeNetworks.md) or [NodeNetworkSet#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.15](NodeNetworkSets.md) with more than one [NodeNetwork#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.12](NodeNetworks.md).
