Association (aka Relation), represents two or more things that are in some way connected.  It is used in [UML#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.3.50](UML.md) as the basis of the other relationship categorization and also essentially a synonym for a [Nodes#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.12.0](../data_structures/abstract/NodeNetworks.md#nodes) in [GraphTheory#1.3.6.1.4.1.33097.1.0.14](../data_structures/abstract/Graphs.md) .
